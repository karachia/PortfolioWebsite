About Me

Hello, my name is Sina, pronounced like "Cena" in JOHN CENA, but spelled differently (duh!).
I am a music composer and software engineer based in Los Angeles, where I recently graduated from USC. I am passionate about the two vast fields of science, and arts, and anywhere they overlap. When I am not busy with coding or writing music, I enjoy drawing, playing piano, kayaking, or checking out new places. I am also a fervent aircraft and aviation enthusiast and enjoy watching and reading about planes. Feel free to shoot me a message or follow me on social media to stay tuned about any upcoming events and performances.
