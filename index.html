About me

Hi! I'm Sina.

Welcome to my portfolio website. I am a Los Angeles based concert music composer as well as a software engineer.
I'm currently studying both fields at University of Southern California.

I am passionate about music, technology, aircraft and aviation, architecture and urban planning, and mass transit.

In my free time, I like to draw, play harmonica and recorder, explore the world in Google Earth, and hang out with my friends.

Feel free to shoot me a message or follow me on social media to stay tuned about any upcoming events and performances.
